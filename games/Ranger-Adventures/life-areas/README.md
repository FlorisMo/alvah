# life-areas/ — the long expansion (7 → 21)

Where we plan growing *Ranger van de Veluwe* from a five-engine **executive-function** game into a
decade-long **life-skills** game. EF is the floor; this is the building on top.

Read in this order:
1. [PLAN.md](PLAN.md) — the master plan: the thesis, the ten areas, the spiral 7→21 model, the
   two tracking models, the open decisions.
2. [research/](research/) — the research pipeline, in three stages (one folder each):
   - [research/research-prompts/](research/research-prompts/) — the seven paste-ready deep-research
     briefs. Grouped so ten areas need only seven prompts without going shallow.
   - [research/research-results/](research/research-results/) — raw Claude Web outputs land here,
     one per brief, unedited.
   - [research/research-conclusions/](research/research-conclusions/) — the **source-of-truth** docs
     distilled from the results; vetted, confidence-flagged, added to over time. The build reads
     from here (the life-areas equivalent of [../research/](../research/) for the EF game).

## The ten areas
Logic & math · Epistemics · Finance & value · Work & agency · Communication · Internal work ·
Creativity & making · Ethics · Health & attention · Perception & the senses. *(See PLAN §2 for why
these, and which are subjects vs capacities.)*
