## 1. Verdict — what we build on

1. **The frameworks are humane and coherent but the evidence is THIN/CONTESTED — and that is
   acceptable *only* because this is self-knowledge, not treatment** (*the honesty tax of the whole
   brief*). IFS rests on essentially one tiny proof-of-concept RCT (Shadick 2013, n=79) plus a small
   null-ish depression study; NVC has thin controlled data; polyvagal neuroanatomy is disputed;
   branded curricula (Zones of Regulation) are "not an evidence-based practice" (2024 review); the
   big school-mindfulness trial (MYRIAD, 8,376 students) was **null and possibly harmful for at-risk
   youth**. We use IFS/NVC/interoception *because Floris finds them true and humane*, **eyes open**,
   never sold as proven.
2. **Two things *are* relatively well-evidenced — lead with them** (*waarschijnlijk solid*):
   **emotional granularity** (a precise feeling vocabulary seeds better regulation — Barrett) and
   **co-regulation** (a calm adult regulates *with* the child before the child can self-regulate —
   Rosanbalm & Murray, the firmest finding in the brief). These ship *first* (§5, §7).
3. **The mechanism is bodily awareness + turning toward parts with curiosity + clean expression —
   plus the parent's co-regulation.** Not cognitive restructuring, not affirmations. Keep regulation
   **phenomenological** (calm/alert/overwhelmed as felt body-states; breath/movement/rest) — never
   teach polyvagal or proprietary "zones" as established fact.
4. **The shared tension loop Floris intuited is real and named in practice** (*practitioner wisdom,
   untrialed in children*): notice a tension (*which part feels it?*) → articulate it cleanly (NVC,
   no blame) → make a request. NVC already contains it as *self-empathy → honest expression*; IFS's
   "U-Turn" and Focusing×NVC "crossing" formalise it. This is the seam with area 4 — **agency owns the
   *project* side of the loop; this brief owns the *inner + relational* side.**
5. **The line that must never move:** recognition + curiosity + (optional) talking to a trusted human
   is the whole permitted scope. **Any work *with* trauma, exiles, unburdening, dissociation, self-harm
   → hard stop, hand to a human.** Tracking never becomes a score. These are values commitments, not
   empirical ones — §8 locks them into the *engine*, not the guidelines.

## 2. What this trains, and why (design intent)

The inner and relational layer: **know your parts and lead them** (IFS), **say the true thing without
a fight** (NVC), and **regulate body + attention** through interoception and nervous-system awareness
(not cognitive restructuring). The AI-era bet: regulation and self-knowledge are irreducible and
can't be outsourced — they are the keel under agency, communication, and learning. Diegetic fit
differs by strand: communication lives natively in-world (farmers, visitors, a frustrated ally), so
it leans **diegetic**; inner work leans **meta-and-private** (the reflective layer about *Alvah's*
inner life stays light, private, unscored); regulation is a **mix** (the ranger needs to steady
himself before a storm in-world; the real habit layer is light meta).

## 3. The spirals 7→21 (three strands)

**Inner work (IFS-style parts + felt sense)**

| Age | Skill | What it is | Flag |
|---|---|---|---|
| 7–8 | "I have parts" | Notice & name one part as a character/animal ("the nervous part") | THIN (IFS); low-risk, intuitive. **Stop at naming.** |
| 9–10 | Parts have good intentions | Each part is trying to help, even clumsily | Practitioner wisdom; humane |
| 11–12 | Holding two parts | "Part of me wants X, part fears Y" — ambivalence, no side-picking | Matches emerging meta-cognition |
| 13–14 | Turning toward with curiosity | Greet a part, get curious, don't fix (Self-energy stance) | Core IFS; **keep non-clinical** |
| 15–16 | Patterns over time | Notice a recurring protector ("the part that goes quiet") | Reflection only; **no unburdening** |
| 17–18 | Self-leadership | Acting from calm/curious Self, not a reactive part | Aspirational; **not a score** |
| 19–21 | Parts in relationships | "U-Turn": check my own part before reacting | Practitioner model; untrialed |

**Communication (NVC + listening + perspective)**

| Age | Skill | What it is | Flag |
|---|---|---|---|
| 7–8 | Fact vs. story | Say what happened without blame; one feeling word | Heavily scaffolded; parent models |
| 9–10 | Feeling + need | "I felt ___ because I needed ___" | Granularity helps; keep concrete |
| 11–12 | Request vs. demand | Ask for something doable; accept "no" | Idealistic without adult modeling |
| 13–14 | Reflective listening | Paraphrase + check before replying | Modest but real evidence |
| 15–16 | Full NVC under heat | 4 steps when someone's angry; anger = unmet need | Hard; needs practice + co-regulation |
| 17–18 | Recursive perspective | What he thinks she thinks; steelman the other | ToM still maturing into adulthood |
| 19–21 | Conflict repair | Re-open after rupture, own your part, restore | Well-regarded; thin formal evidence |

**Regulation & attention (bodily awareness + focus)** — *Health's regulation core; physical-health
basics are §6.*

| Age | Skill | What it is | Flag |
|---|---|---|---|
| 7–8 | Body weather | Notice calm/alert/overwhelmed as body sensations | Interoception link moderate; curricula thin |
| 9–10 | Down-shift tools | Breath, movement, rest — find what works for *me* | Low-risk; **avoid polyvagal claims** |
| 11–12 | Hunger/tired battery | Catch a need before it becomes distress | Practical interoception |
| 13–14 | Single-tasking reps | One thing at a time; notice the pull to switch | Attention mostly developmental by now |
| 15–16 | Attention protection | Engineer-out distraction; felt before/after experiment | Multitasking↔distraction link reasonable |
| 17–18 | Sustained focus blocks | Longer focus tied to genuine interest/goals | Interest > willpower |
| 19–21 | Recovery & rhythm | Sleep/movement as the base layer of focus & mood | Best-evidenced health levers |

## 5. Game mechanics to build (light, mostly private/diegetic)

**Tracking is Model B at its lightest — almost everything here is presence/streak only. There is no
Model A in this brief: nothing about Alvah's inner life is ever scored.** The few "scored"-looking
loops (attention single-tasking) score the *fire*, not the child.

- **The Ranger Meets His Fear Before the Storm** — notice a part with curiosity. Alone at the cabin a
  "scared part" appears as a small creature; greet it, ask what it wants, let it ride along. Scaling:
  8 = name it · 14 = ask its good intention · 18 = lead from calm. *A lantern lights to show **that**
  he paused — never how scared he was.*
- **The Angry Farmer (NVC choice)** — clean expression under heat; options range from blame to a
  fact+feeling+need+ask line. *Notes **whether** he tried a non-blaming line, never its "quality."*
  Diegetic home of the communication strand.
- **Body Weather Vane** — interoception. Optional check before a big choice: calm/alert/overwhelmed
  as wind/sky; more sensation words unlock with age. *A streak of check-ins, no values.*
- **The Two-Voices Bridge** — holding ambivalence; two parts argue at a fork, let both speak before
  choosing. *Presence of "let both speak," not which won.*
- **Felt-Sense Sketchbook** — Gendlin felt sense; draw "where it sits in the body," pick the word
  that fits. Dyslexia-friendly (drawing > text). *Count of entries only.*
- **The U-Turn Gate** — check your own part before reacting; before a heated reply a mirror offers a
  2-second inward glance. Appears later (13+). *A quiet chime, no log of content.*
- **Down-Shift Toolkit** — regulation; discover breath/movement/rest "tools," learn which calms
  *this* body. *Which tools were **tried**, never a calm-score.*
- **Listening Lantern** — reflective listening; paraphrase an NPC's worry to unlock the real
  conversation. *Presence of the paraphrase step.*
- **The Quiet Part** — notice a protector pattern; a companion who goes silent under stress, over
  chapters the player gets curious about why. *Long-arc, presence-only.*
- **Attention Campfire** — single-tasking; tend one fire vs. chasing sparks (notifications), feel
  focus build. *Longest single-task streak, framed as **the fire**, not the child.*
- **Steelman the Stranger** — recursive perspective-taking; state the other character's view so well
  they feel understood; deeper recursion with age. *Completion only.*
- **Repair the Rope Bridge** — conflict repair; after a rupture with an ally, re-open, own your part,
  restore. *That repair was **attempted**.*
- **Fuel Garden (health)** — sleep/movement/food as fuel; rest and eat to have energy for the quest,
  notice the felt difference. *Habit **presence**, never weight/calorie anything.*
- **The Curiosity Compass** — Self-energy stance; a compass that points "toward, with curiosity"
  whenever a part shows up. *Times the compass was **used**.*

**Governing tracking rule across every seed:** *Oordelen zijn voor het getal, niet voor het kind.*
Presence/streak at most. Reflections are private and diegetic by default. **This rule is encoded in
the engine as a hard constraint, not a guideline** (§7 of the build's data layer; brief 6 formalises
the Model-B-light shape so inner-work data *cannot* hold a rating field).

**Build dependencies / gates:**
- **§8 hand-off ("people-thing, not a game-thing") ships before any parts-work.** Non-negotiable
  sequencing.
- The **AI-coach / true-reaction** versions of the Angry Farmer, Listening Lantern, U-Turn etc. depend
  on the §4b simulation engine. **Per PLAN §4b + §7.6: inner-work and anything about Alvah's own
  feelings NEVER go to the cloud — on-device or parent-mediated only.** Lower-sensitivity roleplay
  (a farmer negotiation that is *not* about Alvah's psyche) may use cloud Claude only on Floris's
  explicit opt-in, with safety rails. This split is a build-blocking constraint, not a preference.

## 6. The tension loop & physical-health basics

**The shared tension loop (the seam with area 4).** Scaffold for a child, three beats: (1) **body
check** — *where do I feel it?* (2) **which part is this** — name it, curious not critical; (3) **the
true sentence** — fact + feeling + need + ask. NVC's self-empathy→honest-expression, IFS's U-Turn,
and the Focusing×NVC "crossing" all describe this same move. *Caveat: this integration is practitioner
wisdom, not trialed in children.* [03-agency-projects-deliberate-practice.md](03-agency-projects-deliberate-practice.md)
owns the *project/proposal* end of the same loop (notice→articulate→**act**); this brief owns the
*inner+relational* end (notice→**which part**→clean expression).

**Physical-health basics (deliberately minimal — flagged for a dedicated later pass, PLAN §6).** Frame
as **fuel for the things he wants to do**; never weight/calories, never shame. Highest-leverage,
best-evidenced: **sleep** (consistent bed/wake times; protect from nighttime screens) · **movement**
(WHO ~60 min/day moderate-to-vigorous for school-age; also supports sleep + mood) · **food as fuel**
(regular meals incl. breakfast; notice how the body *feels* before/after — the interoception tie-in —
rather than rules). If user-testing shows this strand is thin, **commission a standalone health brief**
(esp. adolescent nutrition *without* disordered-eating risk).

## 7. Co-regulation — where the PARENT is the mechanism

The most solid finding in the whole brief, and the place the game most deliberately invites Floris in.
Co-regulation (Rosanbalm & Murray) = **warm relationship + coaching/modeling + structured, predictable
environment**, adapted by age; it *precedes and enables* self-regulation, strongest in the early rungs.
The game invites it (never grades the child on it):

- **Two-player "with-me" moments** — *"Find your grown-up and do the breath together"* before a hard
  scene; the parent co-does, doesn't grade.
- **Parent-as-model cards** — the game occasionally asks the parent to share *their own* part/feeling
  out loud (modeling, not performing).
- **Repair scripts for the parent** — short, non-expert prompts for re-connecting after a rough moment
  (own your part, no lecture).
- **A myth-buster for parents** — co-regulation does NOT mean hovering through every storm; it scales
  *down* as the child grows. Over-attachment to "always be beside them" can inhibit self-regulation.
- **Environment, not exhortation** — nudge predictable routines (sleep, one-thing-at-a-time) as the
  family's shared scaffolding.

This is also the *relatedness* lever (SDT) the agency brief flagged: satisfy relatedness through the
parent, not through the app manufacturing social pressure (see [03](03-agency-projects-deliberate-practice.md) §7.7).

## 8. NEVER CROSS THIS LINE — and how to hand off (mandatory)

A game must **not** attempt these, and must hand them to a human, gracefully:

- **Trauma, abuse, exiles, "unburdening," dissociation, flashbacks.** If a "part" reveals real
  pain/memory, the mechanic gently says *"This sounds important and big — this is for a trusted
  person, not the game,"* and surfaces a "talk to your parent / a grown-up you trust" card.
- **Self-harm, suicidal thoughts, harm to others.** Hard stop → caregiver + professional resources;
  never a game choice.
- **Diagnosis or anything that rates the child** ("how anxious / how dysregulated are you").
- **Coercive emotion** ("calm down now," forced gratitude/affirmations).
- **Polyvagal / neuro claims presented as established fact.**
- **Pressure to disclose feelings.** Reflection is *always* optional and private.

**Hand-off design:** a recurring, non-alarming **"This is a people-thing, not a game-thing"** motif so
escalation feels *normal*, not like failure. This motif ships *before* any parts-work, and is part of
the §4b AI-character rails — any generative character hands off the moment a topic gets too real (same
boundary, enforced in the engine).

## 9. Honesty lines — what we must NOT claim or do

- **IFS / NVC / polyvagal / much of SEL are THIN or CONTESTED.** Durlak 2011 (213 SEL programs) found
  *modest, heterogeneous, implementation-dependent* effects (academic g=0.27, SEL-skills g=0.57,
  emotional-distress g=0.24); Cipriano 2023 reaffirms small-to-moderate. Say "humane and coherent,"
  never "proven."
- **No RCT** exists of an interoception curriculum, or of a combined IFS+NVC protocol, **in
  typically-developing children.** The branded curricula (Zones, Mahler) were tested mostly on
  autistic/special-ed samples — extrapolation to a healthy child is not evidence-supported.
- **Conflict of interest** taints several regulation sources (developers studying their own products —
  explicitly Mahler; Zones' strongest "evidence" is vendor-hosted).
- **Mindfulness-for-kids is overclaimed** — MYRIAD was null and possibly *harmful* for at-risk youth.
  Brief body-anchored noticing is fine; universal seated-meditation drills are not.
- **"Name it to tame it" outruns its neuroscience** — pair naming with *being-with* the sensation so
  it doesn't become "explain your feeling away."
- **NVC reliably transforming relationships is idealistic/under-evidenced** — present it as a skill
  that *helps*, not a guarantee.
- **Long-horizon validity is unknown** — none of this is validated as a 7→21 spiral; treat the spiral
  as a humane design *hypothesis*, reviewed as Alvah grows.
- **Dutch from English sources:** the feeling-vocabulary must be re-grounded in Dutch with a native
  check — do **not** machine-translate the emotion words (granularity is the point; a mistranslated
  feeling-word defeats it).

## 10. Accessibility (dyslexia; emotional content read-aloud)

**Read-aloud everything, especially emotional content** — never gate inner/relational learning behind
reading fluency; high-quality TTS + human voice-over for key feeling-words. **Picture-first feeling
vocabulary** — sensations as images/animations (knot, spark, fog) before words. **Multimodal felt
sense** — drawing, movement, colour, sound (leverages Gendlin's non-verbal strength, suits dyslexia).
**No timed reading; no spelling/typing required to express a feeling** — choose from spoken/illustrated
options. **Consistent icons** for the three states and the four NVC steps so the *structure* is
learnable without reading. Treat dyslexia-friendly type as *secondary to audio*. (Consistent with the
EF / area 1–3 a11y line: ≥56px targets, dual-channel feedback, reduced-motion.)
